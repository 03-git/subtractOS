READ OBD-II DIAGNOSTIC CODES
Steps:
1. Get an OBD-II scanner (cheap Bluetooth: ELM327 + Torque app; or handheld unit).
2. Locate OBD-II port under dashboard, driver side, near steering column.
3. Plug in scanner. Turn ignition to ON (do not start engine).
4. Connect app or wait for handheld to initialize.
5. Select "Read Codes" or "Scan." Record all codes (e.g., P0301).
6. Look up codes: Pxxxx = powertrain, Bxxxx = body, Cxxxx = chassis, Uxxxx = network.
7. "Clear Codes" only after recording. If light returns, the fault persists.
Notes:
- Pending codes: intermittent faults not yet triggering the light.
- P0420/P0430 (catalyst efficiency): often O2 sensor, not always the cat.
- Codes point to circuits/systems, not always the failed part. Research before replacing.
