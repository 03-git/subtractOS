ROTATE TIRES
Steps:
1. Loosen all lug nuts 1/4 turn while tires are on the ground.
2. Lift vehicle. Support on 4 jack stands.
3. Front-wheel drive: front tires go straight to rear. Rear tires cross to opposite front.
4. Rear-wheel drive: rear tires go straight to front. Front tires cross to opposite rear.
5. All-wheel drive: X-pattern (each tire crosses to opposite corner).
6. Torque lug nuts to spec in star pattern.
7. Lower vehicle. Recheck torque.
Notes:
- Directional tires (arrow on sidewall): swap front-to-rear same side only.
- Staggered setups (different front/rear sizes): cannot rotate. Just swap left-right if non-directional.
- Rotate per manual schedule or at every oil change.
