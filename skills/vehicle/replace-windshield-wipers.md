REPLACE WINDSHIELD WIPERS
Steps:
1. Lift wiper arm away from windshield. It stays up on a detent.
2. Press release tab on adapter where blade meets arm.
3. Slide old blade off the hook or pin.
4. Slide new blade onto arm until it clicks. Tug to confirm locked.
5. Gently lower arm to windshield. Repeat other side.
6. Test with washer fluid spray.
Notes:
- Lay a towel on the windshield in case the arm snaps back. Cracked windshields happen.
- Replace when streaking or chattering.
- Rear wiper (if equipped) uses a different size. Check fitment guide.
