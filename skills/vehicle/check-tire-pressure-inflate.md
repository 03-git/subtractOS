CHECK TIRE PRESSURE AND INFLATE
Steps:
1. Check tires cold (parked 3+ hours or driven <1 mile).
2. Find target PSI on driver door jamb sticker. Ignore sidewall number (that's max).
3. Remove valve cap. Press gauge firmly onto valve stem. Read PSI.
4. If low: attach air chuck to valve stem. Inflate in short bursts.
5. Recheck with gauge. Release air with gauge pin if overinflated.
6. Repeat all four tires plus spare.
7. Replace valve caps.
Notes:
- Pressure drops in cold weather. Recheck when seasons change.
- TPMS light means pressure is significantly low. Don't wait for the light.
- Check monthly. Underinflation kills fuel economy and causes blowouts.
