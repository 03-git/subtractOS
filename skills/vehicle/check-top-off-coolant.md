CHECK AND TOP OFF COOLANT
Steps:
1. Engine must be COLD. Never open a hot cooling system.
2. Locate coolant reservoir (translucent tank with MIN/MAX marks).
3. Check level against marks. If between MIN and MAX, no action needed.
4. If low: remove reservoir cap. Add premixed coolant to MAX line.
5. If no premixed available: mix concentrate 50/50 with distilled water.
6. Replace cap. Check for leaks around hoses and reservoir.
Notes:
- Use coolant type specified in manual (color alone is not reliable).
- Repeatedly low coolant means a leak. Check under the car for puddles.
- Milky residue on oil cap + low coolant = possible head gasket failure. Stop driving.
