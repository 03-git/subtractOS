CHECK AND REFILL WASHER FLUID
Steps:
1. Open hood. Locate washer fluid reservoir (usually translucent with wiper icon on cap).
2. Check level visually. If low or empty, proceed.
3. Open cap. Pour in washer fluid to fill line (or near top if no line).
4. Replace cap. Close hood.
5. Test spray from cabin controls.
Notes:
- Use actual washer fluid, not plain water. Water grows algae and freezes.
- In cold climates, use winter-rated fluid (de-icer formula).
- If spray is weak or uneven, clean nozzle tips with a pin.
