SEARCHING KIWIX

Ask a factual question ending with ?:
- "what is photosynthesis?"
- "who was Alan Turing?"
- "when was the moon landing?"

Notes:
- Kiwix serves offline Wikipedia. No internet needed.
- Questions ending in ? route to Kiwix automatically.
- "How do I" questions route to skills instead.
- If Kiwix has no answer, the question falls through to the local model.
- Browse Kiwix directly: open a browser to localhost:8888
